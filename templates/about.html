Interactive Map Overview

Explore a dynamic choropleth map created using leaflet.js and D3.js. This map visualizes motor collision frequencies across New York City by zip code, with color intensities indicating accident hotspots.

Interactive zones on the map feature custom popups with key statistics, and links to detailed infographics for each zip code, providing a more granular analysis of collision data.

The map is complemented by a live statistics table, updated in real-time via Flask API calls. The integration of Plotly.js offers an interactive 'Crash Count Over Time' chart, revealing monthly trends against average counts.

Infographic Insights by Zipcode

Delve into detailed visualizations of motor collision statistics in New York City. Interactive charts and graphs, powered by Chart.js and Plotly.js, bring to life data unique to each selected zipcode.

Discover insights on injuries, fatalities, and primary contributing factors of accidents. These complex datasets are presented in an easily digestible format, enhancing accessibility and understanding.

The impact of weather on accidents is depicted through innovative boxplot visualizations, offering an analytical perspective on the relationship between environmental factors and collision occurrences.

Graphical representations of traffic accident distributions across time frames highlight peak accident hours and prevalent patterns, providing valuable temporal insights for safety analysis.

Engage with custom popups embedded in the map, offering zip code-specific statistics and guiding you to a deeper narrative of traffic patterns and safety challenges.

Analysis Board Overview

The Analysis Board presents a holistic dashboard, aggregating diverse metrics such as time, weather conditions, and contributing factors, to paint a comprehensive picture of motor collision trends in New York City.

Examine the nuanced interplay between accidents and weather through detailed boxplots, offering insights into how environmental variables influence collision rates.

Uncover patterns in traffic incidents with in-depth temporal data analyses, identifying key times for potential enhancement of road safety measures.

Dive into analyses that reveal correlations between temperature, wind levels, and accident occurrences, showcasing a multifaceted approach to urban safety.

Engage with user-centric interactive elements, from zoomable maps to clickable charts, each leading to detailed infographics for an enriched data exploration experience.
